Privacy Policy

Your privacy is a priority. Here's exactly what we collect — and what we don't.

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Excusely is designed with your privacy as a priority. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to being transparent about our practices.

What We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our service: your Apple ID (a unique anonymous identifier) to sync your data across devices. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifying information unless you explicitly provide it.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, microphone input, camera data, or any other sensitive information. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

iCloud Sync

Your excuses, scripts, usage history, and preferences are stored using Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services. This data is tied to your Apple ID and syncs securely across your devices. Apple's privacy policy governs how this data is stored and protected.

Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Support page and get in touch.
